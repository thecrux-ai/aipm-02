ABOUT THE COMPANY

Kirana360 Technologies builds inventory management and order management software for mid-to-large retail chains across India. Our platform is used by 400+ retail brands — from regional grocery chains to national pharmacy networks. We are a Series B company (raised ₹120Cr in 2023), headquartered in Pune with offices in Mumbai and Bengaluru.

We are growing our Customer Success team to support our expansion into the Mumbai market and a new set of enterprise retail clients onboarding in Q3 2026.

---

ROLE OVERVIEW

As a Customer Success Manager, you will own the post-sales relationship for 40–60 mid-market and enterprise accounts. You are responsible for ensuring these accounts get measurable value from the Kirana360 platform — leading to high retention, expansion, and referrals.

This is not a support role. You will be expected to understand each customer's business deeply, proactively surface adoption risks before they become churn risks, and drive executive-level relationships at key accounts.

---

WHAT YOU WILL DO

Account Management
- Own the health score and renewal forecast for a portfolio of 40–60 accounts
- Conduct quarterly business reviews (QBRs) with key stakeholders — including C-suite at enterprise accounts
- Identify at-risk accounts based on usage data and engagement signals; develop and execute save plans
- Manage the renewal and expansion motion in partnership with the Account Executive team

Onboarding and Adoption
- Lead structured onboarding for new accounts: kickoff, configuration review, go-live, and 30/60/90 day check-ins
- Drive feature adoption — track usage metrics by account and proactively introduce features aligned to each customer's use case
- Create account-specific success plans with measurable outcomes

Product and Internal Collaboration
- Serve as the voice of the customer in product discussions — aggregate feedback, identify patterns, and escalate high-priority requests with supporting data
- Work with the support team to ensure escalated issues are resolved within SLA
- Partner with Sales on expansion opportunities identified through CSM relationships

---

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR

Must-have
- 3–6 years of experience in customer success, account management, or client servicing for a B2B SaaS company
- Demonstrated experience managing a portfolio of SMB or mid-market accounts (50+ accounts at any point)
- Strong written and verbal communication skills in English; Hindi is a plus for working with certain client segments
- Proven track record of managing renewals and driving retention in a quota-carrying or success-metric-bearing role
- Comfortable working with data: reading dashboards, pulling reports, translating usage data into account health insights

Nice-to-have
- Experience in retail, FMCG, logistics, or supply chain domain — understanding the customer's world is a significant advantage
- Familiarity with CRM tools: Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, or similar
- Experience working directly with product teams to relay customer feedback and influence the roadmap
- Hindi fluency (spoken and written)

Not required
- Engineering or technical background
- MBA or specific educational qualification — we evaluate on demonstrated skills, not credentials

---

WHAT SUCCESS LOOKS LIKE IN YEAR ONE

- Net Revenue Retention (NRR) of >110% across your portfolio
- 0 preventable churns (churns where we had signals but didn't act)
- >80% of your accounts complete QBR within the quarter
- 3+ documented case studies or expansion stories from your accounts
