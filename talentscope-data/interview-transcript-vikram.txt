[CANDIDATE RESPONSE 4]

The hardest adjustment has been the time horizon. In field sales, success was weekly. Did I hit my targets this week? Closed or didn't close. You get feedback fast and you know where you stand.

I'm still calibrating to that. I've started making a rule for myself: one "quiet account" proactive outreach per day. Not a check-in for the sake of it, but something specific — "I noticed you started using the new settlements feature, wanted to see if you had questions." It's helping me build the proactive muscle.
